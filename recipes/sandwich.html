Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is a food typically consisting of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between slices of bread,

Ingredients

  • Bread slices.
  • Cheese slices or cheese sliced from a block.
  • Mayonnaise sauce.
  • to taste Salt.
  • to taste Black pepper.
  • Mustard.
  • Lettuce leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Slather your condiments on the bottom slice of bread.
  2. Add the densest vegetables, like pickles, followed by the lettuce and then the tomato.
  3. Add the cheese.
  4. Add the meat.
  5. Spread another layer of condiments on the top slice of bread.
